Security

Physical nodes offer a higher level of security compared to cloud-based nodes, as they are not subject to the same risks of hacking and data breaches that cloud-based systems are. By running a physical node, you can help to ensure the security of the blockchain network and protect the assets of your customers.

Decentralization

Physical nodes help to support the decentralized nature of blockchain networks, by providing additional points of access and ensuring that the network is not dependent on a single centralized point of control.

Reliability

Physical nodes are more reliable than cloud-based nodes, as they are not subject to the same risks of downtime and outages. By running a physical node, you can help to ensure that the blockchain network is always available and accessible.

Accessibility

By offering IoT devices, Project Pi makes it easier for individuals and businesses to access and use blockchain networks, regardless of their technical expertise. This can help to increase the adoption of blockchain technology and make it more accessible to a wider range of users.
