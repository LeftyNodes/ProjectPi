Presearch

Presearch is a different kind of search engine that works using blockchain technology. It's designed to be more private, fair and community-driven than search engines like Google. With Presearch, you can earn tokens for using the search engine, joining discussions, and sharing the platform with others. You can also personalize your search results by choosing different search providers and filters. Presearch is open to anyone who wants to help make it better, and you can use it to find what you're looking for on the internet while protecting your privacy.
